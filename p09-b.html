The Hellfire Club was rare in that it was open to men and women. It operated out of a cave system underneath a church. The caves were said to be decorated in phallic and erotic symbols. There were rumors of occult rituals, sacrifices, and group sex parties. Franklin described his stays at Dashwood's estate as "paradise". There is even a cave named after him.
