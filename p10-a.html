The Rothschild family gained their wealth in the early 1800s when 5 sons were sent to 5 different European cities to set up banks simultaneously. As their wealth accumulated, they used their power to influence governments, wars, and national debts across the continent. They financed both sides of the Napoleonic Wars. In the mid-1800s, they had the largest private fortune in the world.
