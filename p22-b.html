The Demidoff Table

Art is defined by the emotions it provokes and the meaning the viewer takes away. Look closely. A child is seemingly asleep, laid across the lap of the figure silencing us. A bowl spills from his hand, pouring out an unknown liquid. There is another child, hidden in the back, that holds a compass, which is literally the Freemason logo. This object was placed here in the middle of this room intentionally.
