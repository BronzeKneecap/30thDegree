Egyptian symbology is highly respected by secret societies, who use them for their own beliefs. In the Egyptian rites, Osiris is brutally murdered, his body dismembered and scattered, then reassembled and resurrected by Isis. This practice is duplicated in the Freemason's 3rd rite, where they roleplay a candidate's death and resurrection.
