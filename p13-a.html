The Intercession of Christ and the Virgin
Gallery 305 · Ground Floor
◈

While this painting was created before Freemasonry, which was created in the 1700s, it features many of the same symbols. God, at the top, is posed in a triangular posture, with a halo around him, much like the Eye of Providence. God, the Virgin Mary, and Jesus form a triangle in their composition.
